Information for Students and Post-Docs

 
 
Post-Doc possitions: If you are based in Germany and would like to work as a post-doctoral researcher with me, please send me an email at schapos@uic.edu. The Alexander von Humboldt foundation has a program through which you could come to UIC for 1~2 years. 


Students: If you’re a student at UIC and looking for research in my area, don’t hesitate to get in touch by emailing me at schapos@uic.edu.


For some global as well as particular open problems in my area, students may enjoy reading this from one of the workshops I organized.


Funding for Students and Post-Docs information:

  • for US students: NSF graduate fellowships.
  • for US and non-US students: Fulbright fellowships.
  • for German students: DFG research grants.
  • for short visits, any student or Post-Doc: GEAR graduate internship.
